The Vision

A Sanctuary for Spiritual Growth and Community Service

"Our new building isn't just about brick and mortar; it's about creating a space where the weary find rest, the seeking find truth, and our community finds a home. We are building a lighthouse for the Makeni region."

This ambitious project incorporates a 1,200-seat sanctuary, dedicated youth education wings, and a multi-purpose community hall designed to host outreach programs and health expos. Every architectural detail has been chosen to reflect our commitment to reverence, excellence, and the welcoming spirit of Christ.
