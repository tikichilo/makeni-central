The Promise of Restoration

"He restores my soul; He leads me in the paths of righteousness for His name's sake." — Psalm 23:3

This week, we explore the transformative power of God's restorative love. Through the study of the life of David, we see how even in our deepest valleys, the Good Shepherd is working to bring us back to wholeness. Join us as we dive into the depths of grace and the certainty of His guidance.

— John 15:5

Our Mission & Values

We are committed to sharing the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ in the context of the three angels' messages of Revelation 14:6-12. Our purpose is to nurture a diverse community through scripture-led worship, compassionate service, and intentional spiritual growth.

At Makeni Central, we believe that every individual is a valued child of God, and we strive to create an environment where faith flourishes and lives are transformed for eternity.
